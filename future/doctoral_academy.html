What we do

Our aim is to provide postgraduate researchers every opportunity to grow in confidence, intellectual capacity, ambition and creativity. The Doctoral Academy works alongside Schools, Faculties and Professional Services to harmonise the quality of the postgraduate research experience across the University. Our core responsibilities include:

  • delivering an excellent Researcher Development Programme
  • sharing good practice in supervision
  • signposting specialist support for postgraduates across the University
  • quality assurance and monitoring of student progress
